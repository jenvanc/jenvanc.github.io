Baking Basics!

Did you know that bakers are really chemists? That iconic white uniform is really a lab coat, because chemical reactions are taking place all day long in their kitchens! Cakes, cookies, brownies and all your other favorite sweet treats are just a chemistry experiment in your own kitchen. Your life long dream of being a mad scientist is finally coming true!

Now, since baking is chemisty, there are a lot of reactions that are happening to take that soft squishy dough and turn it into something that holds its shape and has a wonderful fluffy texture.
